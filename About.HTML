About

Our Photography services is a collaboration between Keegan Flahive and Lauren Esh. 
We are a small photography business located in Lititz, Pennsylvania.
We are here to capture pictures that will last a lifetime.
We specialize in aerial, portrait, and landscape photography. 
Take a look below to learn a little more about Keegan and Lauren.


Keegan Flahive

I am currently a Senior at Harrisburg University where I study Geo-spatial technology (GIS).
I have been working for my dads business ( Argos UAS ) as drone pilot since 2016 and have
conducted hundreds of projects utilizing photography both in the air and on the ground. I spend
my free time traveling and photographing everything I can with my camera and drone.
 Love to watch college football and play video games with friends when its a rainy day and I cannot be outside.
